AUTUMN SEASON

5 Reasons why Autumn season is the best season

1. The trees change color and look really pretty - yes with the changes in temperature you will notice the leaves starting to turn a beautiful orange and yellow welcoming the changing season.

2. You can enjoy autumn season flavors with pumpkin spiced lattes and other pumpkin-flavored food - pumpkin flavors are always a favorite for everyone, and with the changing season, you can really taste new and exciting things that are perfect for everyone to enjoy!

3. There are plenty of leaves to kick - because of the falling leaves, it gives you more reason to have fun with friends kicking the leaves up in the air.

4.It's the season for the best rainy weather - during the autumn season rain is a signature sign fall has begun, and it gives you a chance to dance in the rain.

5. Mornings are golden and may be misty - the autumn mornings bring a wonderful misty feel that is quiet and alluring for morning walkers.
